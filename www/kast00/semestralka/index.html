About the project

Cashless VŠE is a project focused on helping students find and help to use all places where you can pay without cash inside school campus. The goal is to provide all information about restaurants, shops and vending machines.

Yes, restaurants and shops are easy, they are changing maybe once in decade. But vending machines are more complicated because there are so many of them and they are changing constantly. Some of them are cashless but not always working. That is why we give you option to report new cashless vending machines or to report some that are not working.

We hope that this website will help you when you are in the rush and you don't have cash on you.

You probably won't be happy as this lady from photobank but still you will discover where you can pay without cash. And that is something!

Ekopress Bookshop

This bookshop is located in VŠE campus, old building, ground floor.

It offers books about economy, management, accounting, marketing, psychology, personal development, career etc. You can also find language textbooks, foreign publications and professional periodicals.

Scripts, publications for VŠE lectures and promotionals items of university can also be bought in this shop.

Store accepts credit/debit cards.
