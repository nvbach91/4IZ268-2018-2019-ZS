Who is this person?

  • Sebastian teaches English. He teaches private one-to-one lessons via Skype and in person in Prague, the Czech Republic. He also teaches groups in companies.
  • Sebastian is a bilingual native speaker in English. He also considers Czech as his native language and loves learning and speaking German, French or Italian.

What is different about his lessons?

  • You get structured feedback about all aspects of your English to your email address after each lesson. Coming soon: Web app with all feedback data online!
  • The first lesson is free of charge because we first need to customize the lessons exactly to what you need. Most people need to improve their fluency. Some need certificates. What do you need? Send me an email and tell me. We'll find a way to make it work for you!

Why have lessons with Sebastian?

Usually, people look for private teachers because they need to practice speaking in a foreign language. They pay native speakers to sit in cafés and talk to them for an hour. But ... Is that all? Don't you expect more for your money?

Yes, you can and should expect more!

We both know you need more! You need to know what you should improve! You need a step-by-step strategy, you need a method and you need guidance! You don't need a "guy you can pay to talk to you", you need an actual teacher - a person who cares about your progress! That's me.
