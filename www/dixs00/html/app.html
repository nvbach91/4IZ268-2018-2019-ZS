Practice vocabulary using spaced repetition!

Choose a topic from the dropdown menu and press "practice".
  1. Once you choose a topic and click the "Practice" button, words shall be stored in your browser's local storage
  2. You can then practice the words: Look at the word and try to remember the translation.
  3. Was it easy or hard? Or have you perhaps forgotten? Doesn't matter! Click the "Show answer" button
  4. Choose the appropriate button to rate how well you remembered the word.
  5. The "Good" button increases this word's "level of practice" by one.
  6. The "Forgot" button DEcreases this word's "level of practice" by one.
  7. The "Hard" button leaves the level of practice of this word as it was before.
  8. The "Easy" button increases this word's "level of practice" by two.
  9. The app remembers how much you have practiced each word.
  10. Each time you practice a word, the next word will be the one which has the lowest practice level
