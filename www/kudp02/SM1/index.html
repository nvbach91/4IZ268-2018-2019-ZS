Iceland

Meetup at Keflavik airport, arrival time is flexible. It will be a calm adaptation day including walks in the city center and in the port, a visit to hot springs and watching the sunset on the waterfront overlooking the monument of Drakkar. We will spend the first night at Reykjavik’s camping site.

Tuscany

We will photograph the sunrise near the town of Mucigliano, surrounded by classic Tuscan landscapes, yet not very famous among tourists and photographers. Then we will drive along Crete Senese, one of the most beautiful roads of Tuscany.

Provence

We will move to Valensol plateau, the lavender universe! On our way, we will visit Avignon, a historic city famous for captivity of popes, and enjoy the majestic Pope’s Palace.
